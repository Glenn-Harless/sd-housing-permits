# San Diego Development & Housing Permits Pipeline

## Overview
Track where San Diego is building by analyzing development permit data. Visualize construction activity, solar adoption, and permit approval timelines to assess whether the region is on track to meet its housing goals (~172K new homes by 2029).

## Key Questions
- Is permit activity tracking toward the regional housing goal?
- Which neighborhoods are growing fastest?
- How long do permits take to approve, and does it vary by area?
- What does the solar adoption curve look like by neighborhood?

## Available Datasets
- **Development Permits**: https://data.sandiego.gov/datasets/development-permits-set1/
  - Approvals for new construction, additions, remodeling, electrical/mechanical/plumbing
  - Processed by Development Services Department
- **Solar/Expedited Permits**: Available via the same portal
  - Expedited after Climate Action Plan passage
- **County Housing Data**: https://www.sandiegocounty.gov/content/sdc/housing-hub/data-and-insights.html
  - Affordable housing tracking, RHNA progress

## Data Access
CSV downloads from city open data portal. No API key required.
